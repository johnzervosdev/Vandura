## Agent Reading Guide

- **Starting a new task?** Read `van/project.md` first, then the story in `van/stories.md`.
- **B.A. starting a story:** `van/project.md` (current status + next actions) → `van/stories.md` (AC for your story)
- **Murdock running QA:** `van/stories.md` (QA checklist for the story) → `van/qa.md` (record results)
- **Hannibal reviewing:** `van/project.md` only, unless diving into a specific story or QA result
